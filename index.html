AI & Industry Control Laboratory

Laboratory Introduction

Welcome to the AI & Industry Control Lab at the Mongolian University of Science and Technology (MUST). Our research focuses on bridging the gap between theoretical electrical system automation and practical, modern hardware implementation.

We provide a stimulating workspace for students to experiment with smart grid technologies, edge artificial intelligence, and autonomous robotics.

Lab Policies & General Rules

  • Safety First: High-voltage equipment and inverters must only be operated following strict safety protocols.
  • Equipment Usage: Hardware modules (Raspberry Pi, Sensors, iRobot) must be handled with care and returned to their designated stations.
  • Data Management: All IoT and telemetry data should be routed through the lab's secure local network.
  • Workspace: Maintain a clean and organized environment for all ongoing research themes.
